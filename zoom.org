In order to demonstrate achievements on IOOPM, you need to have the Zoom client installed. 
This is necessary for using your web camera so that we can see your face. 
#+end_important

* How to Get Hold of Your Zoom Number <<number>>

We need your Zoom Number so that we can pair you together with examiners for demonstration. 
Below are the steps needed to get hold of your 10-digit Zoom number. You should enter this
number in the AU Portal software when you login for the first time.

* How to disable passwords and waiting rooms

Open your profile by following the instructions above to get to
your Zoom number. Then, click the settings button in the browser.

#+NAME: fig_zoom_7
#+ATTR_HTML: :width 75%
#+CAPTION: In your profile, access Settings
[[./images/zoom-passcode1.png]]

Then turn off your passcodes and your waiting room. You can enable
these for individual meetings in the future if you want, so don't
worry.
